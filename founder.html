Doğukan Yegin

Founder & Chairman | Tech Entrepreneur

Doğukan Yegin is a visionary technology entrepreneur, game developer, and cybersecurity expert based in Sweden. With a career built on innovation and digital systems, he has turned his focus toward one of the most pressing social issues of our time: Neurodiversity Rights.

Believing that the logic of technology can be applied to solve social challenges, Yegin founded the Autism Awareness Party of Sweden as a non-electoral platform to drive systemic change.

A Modern Approach to Advocacy

Unlike traditional politicians, Yegin approaches advocacy with the mindset of an engineer: identifying bugs in the social system and patching them with policy solutions. His leadership ensures that the party remains independent, data-driven, and focused on results rather than rhetoric.

Personal Commitment

"We are not here to play politics. We are here to build a future where every mind is valued. This is not just a party; it is a movement for cognitive liberty."
