Privacy Policy (GDPR)

Last updated: 2026-01-13

1. Purpose of This Policy

Autism Awareness Party of Sweden respects the privacy of individuals and processes personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This policy explains what personal data is processed, for what purpose, and what rights individuals have.

2. Data Controller

Autism Awareness Party of Sweden
Email: kontakt@dindomän.se

3. Personal Data We Process

We process only minimal and voluntarily provided personal data, which may include: name, email address, country of residence, and optional information provided in membership or contact communications. We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data.

4. Purpose of Processing

Personal data is processed exclusively for handling membership applications, internal communication related to the party’s activities, and responding to inquiries. Personal data is never used for commercial purposes, profiling, or political campaigning.

5. Legal Basis

Processing is based on explicit consent given by the individual (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR), or legitimate interest limited to internal administration and communication (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the stated purposes. Membership-related data is deleted upon termination of membership or upon request.

7. Data Sharing and Transfers

Personal data is not shared with third parties, not sold, and not transferred for advertising or political campaigning. Digital communication and storage may involve service providers located outside Sweden; such processing is conducted in accordance with GDPR safeguards.

8. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are applied to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Access is restricted to authorized persons only.

9. Your Rights

Individuals have the right to request access, correction, and deletion of their personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. Requests can be made by contacting the data controller via the email address above.

10. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, tracking technologies, or analytics tools that collect personal data.

11. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if required by law or changes in operations. The current version is always published on this website.

12. Contact

If you have questions regarding personal data or this policy, please contact: kontakt@dindomän.se
