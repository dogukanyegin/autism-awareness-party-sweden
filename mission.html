Our Mission & Vision

Building a society where neurodiversity is a strength, not a barrier.

The Manifesto

The Autism Awareness Party of Sweden was founded on a simple yet powerful premise: Neurodiversity is a natural and valuable part of human variation.

For too long, autistic individuals have been viewed through a lens of deficit. We are here to change that narrative. We are here to advocate for a society that adapts to its citizens, rather than forcing citizens to adapt to a rigid mold.

Our Strategic Goals

  • Systemic Inclusion: Moving beyond "awareness" to "acceptance" and "integration" in schools and workplaces.
  • Legal Protection: Ensuring that neurodivergent traits are protected under Swedish anti-discrimination laws.
  • Healthcare Access: Reducing wait times for diagnosis and ensuring evidence-based support systems.
  • Public Dialogue: Normalizing neurodiversity in media, politics, and culture.
