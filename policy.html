Policy & Research

Our proposals are based on data, medical research, and human rights frameworks.

1. Inclusive Education

We advocate for a reform in the Swedish school system to provide personalized learning environments for neurodivergent students, moving away from the "one size fits all" model.

2. Employment Accessibility

We propose tax incentives for companies that implement "Neurodiversity-Friendly" hiring practices and workspace adjustments, tapping into a vast, underutilized talent pool.

3. Early Diagnosis

Reducing the waiting times for autism and ADHD assessments in Sweden is a priority. Early intervention leads to better life outcomes and reduced social costs.

4. Legal Recognition

We work to ensure that sensory processing sensitivities are recognized as valid reasons for accommodation in public spaces and government institutions.
